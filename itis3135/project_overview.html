Project Overview

Project Name: UrbanThreadz Apparel

Function / Purpose: UrbanThreadz Apparel is a streetwear fashion brand founded by Nathan Tegegne, a young designer passionate about using fashion to make a positive impact in the world. The website’s purpose is to showcase Nathan’s clothing designs, highlight his message of creativity and social impact, and allow users to interactively explore outfits and style ideas.

Intended Users: Teens and young adults interested in streetwear fashion, fans of sustainable and socially-conscious clothing, and potential collaborators, models, or photographers.

Overview of the Content: The website will include pages for Home, Shop, Lookbook, About, and Contact, featuring brand information, product catalog, galleries, bio, and forms for inquiries or collaborations.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Home Page: Rotating banner for featured collections using JS/jQuery.
  • Shop Page: Filter products dynamically (type, size, color) using JavaScript.
  • Lookbook Page: Interactive gallery with modal popups (Lightbox or similar plugin).
  • Contact Page: Validate form fields before submission using JavaScript/jQuery.
