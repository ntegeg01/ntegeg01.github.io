Website Evaluations

Lusion

Lusion is a design agency website known for immersive 3D visuals and smooth interactive experiences.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast between text and background makes important content stand out.
  • Repetition: Animations, typography, and colors are applied consistently across the site.
  • Alignment: Grid-based layout keeps elements neat and visually balanced.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together, which improves readability and flow.

Ling’s Cars

Ling’s Cars is a car leasing site that overwhelms users with chaotic visuals, excessive animations, and poor organization.

  • Contrast: Bright, clashing colors and random font choices make it hard to focus.
  • Repetition: Design elements are inconsistent, leading to a confusing experience.
  • Alignment: Poorly aligned sections and scattered placement reduce readability.
  • Proximity: Unrelated elements are placed too close, creating visual clutter and disorganization.
